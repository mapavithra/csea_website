About CSEA Association

The Computer Science Engineering Association (CSEA) is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting the academic and professional development of students in the field of Computer Science and Engineering.

Our mission is to foster a collaborative environment where students can engage in technical projects, workshops, competitions, and social activities to enhance their skills and network with professionals in the industry.

We organize regular events such as hackathons, coding challenges, guest lectures, and industrial visits to keep our members updated with the latest trends and technologies in the field of computer science.
